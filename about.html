About Hi, I'm Himanshu

AWS Certified Solutions Architect, ScrumMaster & Mentor. Expert in Tech Project Delivery.

I am an AWS Certified Solutions Architect and Certified ScrumMaster® with over 20 years of experience in system analysis, project management, and web development. Currently based in Canada, I’ve had the opportunity to collaborate with clients across the globe to deliver cutting-edge, scalable digital solutions. I specialize in leading agile-driven technical projects, driving innovation, and ensuring seamless collaboration to achieve impactful business outcomes. My extensive experience in managing complex projects has allowed me to make meaningful contributions to the IT and development sectors.

How I Got Here

My journey to where I am today has been shaped by a passion for technology and a relentless drive to solve complex challenges. I hold a Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology, which laid the foundation for my career in system analysis, project management, and web development. Starting my career as a developer in a small team of just two, I quickly realized my aptitude for understanding systems and finding efficient solutions. Over the years, I’ve grown both technically and as a leader, eventually managing teams of up to 20 developers. Along the way, I also ran my own company, which allowed me to further hone my entrepreneurial skills and deepen my understanding of business needs. I became an AWS Certified Solutions Architect and a Certified ScrumMaster®, which further enhanced my ability to lead agile-driven projects. Working with clients worldwide has expanded my skill set, allowing me to innovate and deliver scalable, impactful solutions. Today, I specialize in helping businesses navigate the complexities of the digital landscape, delivering results that exceed expectations and drive lasting success.
