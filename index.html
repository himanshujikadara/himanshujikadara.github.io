About

AWS Certified Solutions Architect, ScrumMaster & Mentor. Expert in Tech Project Delivery.

I am an AWS Certified Solutions Architect and Certified ScrumMaster® (CSM®) with over 20 years of experience in system analysis, project management, and web development. Originally from India and currently based in Canada, I have had the privilege of working with clients across Canada, the USA, Europe, and Hong Kong. My diverse skill set enables me to deliver innovative, results-driven solutions that help businesses optimize their digital strategies and systems.

With a strong background in agile methodologies and software engineering, I specialize in leading technical projects from planning through execution. My focus is on delivering high-quality results while fostering cross-functional team collaboration and effective communication. Whether it's project planning, technical execution, or user training, I ensure seamless delivery and client satisfaction.

Whether you need a Technical Project Manager, Solutions Architect, Fractional CTO, Full Stack Developer, Software Engineer, or ScrumMaster®, I bring the expertise to build high-performing websites, applications, and products. I’m also available for consultation or mentoring opportunities, aiming to empower individuals and organizations to grow and succeed. Let’s connect and work together to drive your business or cause forward.
